Welcome to Jhon's House!

The Pro Shop Our Pro Shop is stocked with the latest tennis gear, from rackets to shoes and accessories. Our expert staff can help you choose the right equipment and offer racket stringing services to keep you playing at your best. Tournaments and Events We host a variety of tournaments, leagues, and social mixers for all levels. Whether you're a competitive player or just looking for a fun match, there’s always something happening at Jhon’s House. Tennis for All Ages From juniors to seniors, we offer programs for all ages and skill levels. Our beginner classes, development programs, and advanced clinics ensure that everyone can enjoy the game.
