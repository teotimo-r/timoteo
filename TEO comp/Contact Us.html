Lo De Jhon

Lo De Jhon is the perfect spot for both tennis enthusiasts and food lovers. We offer top-quality tennis gear—rackets, shoes, apparel, and accessories—along with expert advice to help you choose the best equipment. After your game, relax with a variety of delicious meals and drinks, including fresh sandwiches, smoothies, and specialty coffee. With a cozy, social atmosphere, our café is ideal for refueling, chatting with fellow players, or catching up on work. We also host tennis events and clinics to bring the community together. Come for the game, stay for the food!
